# ReLoop — 4-Minute Demo Video Script

> **Live:** https://reloop-woad.vercel.app · **Tagline:** *"The landfill is a design flaw."*
> A 4:00 guided flow that covers every feature: the four pillars (AI Grading,
> Smart Routing, Product Health Card, Prevention) across the **user app** and the
> **seller dashboard**.

---

## Before you hit record (2-min setup)

- **Warm the API.** Render's free tier sleeps — open the site and run one Sell
  grading *once* before recording so the first AI call isn't a 50s cold start.
- **Browser:** full-screen, hide the bookmarks bar, 100% zoom, close other tabs.
- **Accounts you'll use** (login = type a handle, no password):
  - `meera` — shopper (Pune). Has the staged 2-life item + a return-eligible item.
  - `aarav` — shopper (Bengaluru). Has the Listing Agent / recycle listing.
  - `techbazaar` — seller dashboard.
- **Pre-stage tabs** (optional but smooth): one tab per major stop so you cut
  instead of waiting on navigation.
- **Recording:** 1080p+, cursor highlight on, system audio off. Record VO
  separately or read live — lines below are timed for ~140 wpm.

**Legend:** 🎬 = on-screen action · 🎙 = voiceover · 💡 = director's note

---

## 0:00 – 0:18 · The hook (Landing page)

🎬 Open on **https://reloop-woad.vercel.app**. Slow-scroll the hero → the four
pillars row → the "live price trace / routing decision" panel.

🎙 *"Every year, billions of returned and unused products get hauled back to
warehouses or written off. ReLoop is the AI layer that gives them a second life
— and decides their best next path before they ever move. It's customer-obsessed,
one-stop, and Amazon-native. Four pillars make it work: AI grading, smart routing,
a product health card, and prevention."*

💡 Let the "Refurbish → Resell" routing card and the ₹ price trace land on screen
— that's your thesis in one frame.

---

## 0:18 – 0:30 · One login, many lives (Multi-account)

🎬 Click **Get Started** → on **/login**, hover the identity cards, click **Meera**.

🎙 *"One account model spanning shoppers and sellers. I'll start as Meera — a
shopper. No passwords, just pick who you are."*

💡 Show the account cards briefly so reviewers see it's a real multi-user demo.

---

## 0:30 – 0:52 · Pillar 4: Prevention (Store)

🎬 Go to **Store** (buy-new). Open any product detail — point at the
**return-likelihood / "get it right the first time"** prediction.

🎙 *"The best return is the one that never happens. Before you even buy, ReLoop
predicts how likely this exact choice is to come back — wrong size, wrong fit —
and nudges you to the right pick. That's prevention: stopping the waste at the
source."*

💡 This is the pillar most demos forget — lead with it to stand out.

---

## 0:52 – 1:28 · Pillars 1 + 2: Grade at the source, decide before it moves (Return)

🎬 Go to **My Items** → **Eligible for return** tab → **Return this item**.
Walk the flow: **Reason → Grading (doorstep) → Intelligent Bridge → Handoff.**

🎙 *"Here's the core innovation. When Meera returns an item, our AI grades it
right at the doorstep — from photos, before it moves an inch. Then the Intelligent
Bridge — a transparent, rules-based engine — weighs value, local handling cost,
nearby demand, and carbon, and picks the best path: resell locally, refurbish,
donate, recycle, or warehouse. The model narrates; the logic decides — every
call is auditable. The item skips the warehouse round-trip entirely."*

💡 Pause on the **Intelligent Bridge** screen — the factor breakdown is the
"glass-box AI" money shot. Let the chosen path animate in.

---

## 1:28 – 1:58 · Pillar 1 in depth + the trust layer (Sell)

🎬 **My Items** → **Resell** tab → **Sell this item** → upload the staged photos
→ watch **Grading** (with the *"matches the original listing"* reference check) →
**Price** → **Health Card** issued. Land on the EcoCredits + CO₂ payoff.

🎙 *"Selling is just as smart. The AI grades condition and even diffs the photos
against the original Amazon listing to verify it's authentic and unchanged. It
sets a fair price, and mints a Product Health Card — verifiable condition,
issues, authenticity — plus the EcoCredits and CO₂ you just saved."*

💡 Zoom on the **Health Card** as it's stamped — that's the trust artifact.

---

## 1:58 – 2:35 · Pillar 3: A Health Card that travels — "CARFAX for a product" (Shop)

🎬 Go to **Shop** → open the staged **Adidas Ultraboost** detail. Scroll to
**"This item's lives"**. Then open **Rufus** chat and ask *"Is this worth it?"*.

🎙 *"This is our moat. The Health Card doesn't belong to a listing — it travels
with the physical item across owners. Look: this pair was born at Amazon, owned
by Aarav, graded, sold to Meera — and now she's relisting it. Two lives, one
verified chain, with the total CO₂ saved across its whole life. Think CARFAX, but
for a product — and only Amazon can prove a lineage from first sale. Buyers can
even ask Rufus, our health-card-aware assistant, anything before they commit."*

💡 The **two-life timeline** + the cumulative-impact header is the single most
impressive frame in the app. Hold it for a beat.

---

## 2:35 – 3:00 · Autonomy: the Listing Agent (My Listings)

🎬 Switch account to **Aarav** (top-right **Switch**). Open **My Listings** →
the **Wired Earphones** listing → press **Advance day / Auto-run**. Show the
**price chart**, **activity feed**, then the **recycle recommendation**.

🎙 *"Every listing gets an autonomous agent. It watches the local market and acts
— repricing toward comparables, widening the search radius, improving the listing.
And when resale just isn't viable, like these worn earphones priced below the
floor, it doesn't sit forever — it recommends recycling to recover materials and
EcoCredits. You can always take the wheel with a manual override."*

💡 Auto-run a few "days" fast so the reprice → recycle arc plays on its own.

---

## 3:00 – 3:15 · Eliminate the loop entirely (Amazon Mesh)

🎬 Go to **Mesh** (hyperlocal lending). Show a couple of lend/borrow listings.

🎙 *"And the most circular idea of all — Amazon Mesh. Rent out the things sitting
idle at home, or borrow what a neighbor already owns instead of buying new. No new
inventory, no warehouse — a return loop eliminated before it starts."*

---

## 3:15 – 3:25 · Rewarded for the loop (Rewards)

🎬 Go to **Rewards** → show the **EcoCredits ledger** and a redeemable voucher.

🎙 *"Every good choice — returning smart, reselling, recycling, lending — earns
EcoCredits you redeem for real value. Sustainability that pays you back."*

---

## 3:25 – 3:52 · The other interface: Seller dashboard

🎬 **Switch** → log in as **techbazaar** → land on **/seller**. Show the donut
(**rescued / refurbished / donated / discarded**) + metrics. Quick montage:
**Returns queue → Rescue pipeline → Bulk exchange → Spare parts.**

🎙 *"For sellers handling returns at volume, the same brain runs a full dashboard.
A live view of what's being rescued, refurbished, donated, or recycled. A returns
queue graded at the doorstep, a rescue pipeline, a B2B bulk-exchange for surplus,
and even spare-part harvesting — pulling value out of items too far gone to
resell whole."*

💡 Keep this a brisk montage — 4–5 seconds per screen. The donut chart animating
in is a strong opener for this segment.

---

## 3:52 – 4:00 · Close

🎬 Cut back to the landing hero: *"The landfill is a design flaw."*

🎙 *"AI that grades at the source, decides before the item moves, and carries
trust to its next owner. Nothing of value ever hits a landfill. That's ReLoop —
closing the loop, the Amazon way."*

💡 End card: logo + **reloop-woad.vercel.app**. Hold 2 seconds on the tagline.
